#### Process Flow for Job Application and Onboarding
**Term:** Process Flow  
**Definition:** A systematic sequence of actions or steps that must be followed to complete a task, in this context, the entire journey from job application submission to onboarding.  
**Formula:** 
- **Search Jobs**: Initial step where candidates look for available positions.
- **Prepare Documents**: Candidates gather necessary documents such as resumes and cover letters.
- **Submit Application**: Documents are submitted through a job portal or direct application process.
- **Confirm Application Submission**: Confirmation of receipt by the employer.
- **Forward Application**: Application may be forwarded to relevant departments or individuals.
- **Review Application**: Human resources or hiring managers review the application.
- **Shortlist Applicants**: Shortlisted candidates proceed further in the selection process.
- **Schedule Interview**: Selected candidates are scheduled for interviews.
- **Attend Interview**: Candidates participate in interviews conducted by the employer.
- **Conduct Interview**: Employer conducts interviews with selected candidates.
- **Await Decision**: Candidates wait for feedback on their interview performance.
- **Make Decision**: Final decision is made to hire or reject a candidate.
- **Create Rejection Email**: If rejected, an email is prepared to inform the candidate.
- **Update Job Portal**: Job portal information is updated based on the hiring status.
- **Send Registration Email**: A registration email is sent to applicants who are shortlisted but not yet interviewed.
- **Send Job Offer**: An offer letter is sent to successful candidates.
- **Decide Offer**: Final decision on whether to accept or reject the job offer.
- **Begin Onboarding**: Successful candidates start their onboarding process.

**Example:**
1. A candidate searches for a job position in the "Search Jobs" step.
2. They prepare necessary documents like resumes and cover letters, which is the "Prepare Documents" step.
3. The candidate submits these documents through an online portal or direct application, marking the "Submit Application" step.
4. Once submitted, they receive confirmation of receipt from the employer, completing the "Confirm Application Submission" step.

This structured process ensures a systematic and organized approach to handling job applications and onboarding new employees.
