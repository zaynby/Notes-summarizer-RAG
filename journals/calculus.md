### Term: [[Limit]]

- **Formula**:  
  $$
  \lim_{x \to a} f(x) = L
  $$  
- **Example**:  
  $$
  \lim_{x \to 2} (3x^2) = 3(2)^2 = 12
  $$

---

### Term: [[Derivative]]  
- **Definition**: A measure of the rate of change of a function with respect to its input.  
- **Formula**:  
  $$
  f'(x) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x+h) - f(x)}{h}
  $$  
- **Example**:  
  $$
  f(x) = x^3 \implies f'(x) = 3x^2
  $$

---

### Term: [[Integral]]

- **Formula**:  
  $$
  \int f(x) \, dx = F(x) + C
  $$  
- **Example**:  
  $$
  \int 2x \, dx = x^2 + C
  $$
